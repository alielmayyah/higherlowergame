How to Play

  1. Use the arrows to guess a number between 1 and 10.
  2. Each click increases or decreases your guess by one.
  3. Click "Guess" to check your guess against the target number.
  4. The game will indicate if the target number is higher or lower than your guess.
  5. Keep guessing until you find the correct number!
