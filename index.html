How to Play

1. Guess a number by clicking the increase or decrease arrows.

2. Single click to increase the guess by one.

3. Click "Guess" to check your guess against the target number.

4. The game will tell you if the target number is higher or lower than your guess.

5. Keep guessing until you find the correct number!
